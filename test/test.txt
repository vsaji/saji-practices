When it comes to internal mobility, hiring managers need to be trained and instructed to engage with internal candidates in an honest and fair manner. In my experience, 90% of the time, hiring managers either falsely claim the role has already been filled or engage with us just to follow protocol, while ultimately filling the position through unofficial channels. I believe there should be clear governance around this process to ensure internal candidates are fairly considered and recognized, which would help Citi retain its top talent
